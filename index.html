Biography

I am a Ph. D. student at Medical Physics Department of University of Wisconsin-Madison advised by Dr. Guang-Hong Chen. My current research projects are related to

  • x-ray photon counting detectors (detector modeling and application)

  • x-ray computed tomography (system design and reconstruction algorithm)

  • x-ray phase contrast imaging.
