Biography

I am a Ph. D. student in Department of Medical Physics at University of Wisconsin-Madison advised by Dr. Guang-Hong Chen. My research projects are related to

  • x-ray photon counting detectors (detector modeling and application)

  • x-ray computed tomography (system design and reconstruction algorithm)

  • x-ray phase contrast imaging.
