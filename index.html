Biography

I am a Ph. D. student in CT research group in Department of Medical Physics at University of Wisconsin-Madison advised by Dr. Guang-Hong Chen. My research projects are related to

  • X-ray photon counting detectors (detector modeling and application)

  • X-ray computed tomography (system design and reconstruction algorithm)

  • X-ray phase contrast imaging
